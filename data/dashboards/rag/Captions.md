## 1. Company Overview
- ClearCaptions, LLC provides captioning services for phone calls, specifically designed for individuals with hearing loss.
- Their core product is a captioned telephone service that displays near real-time captions of conversations on a touchscreen device.
- The target market includes U.S. residents with hearing loss who qualify for the service under the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).
- Founded: Not disclosed; Headquarters: United States.
