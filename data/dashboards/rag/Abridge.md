## 1. Company Overview
- Abridge is a healthcare technology company that specializes in transforming clinical conversations into actionable insights using generative AI.
- Their core product is an AI-powered clinical documentation platform that automates medical note-taking, enhancing clinician efficiency and patient care.
- Abridge primarily targets large healthcare systems and institutions, aiming to improve clinician workflows and patient interactions.
- Founded in 2020, the company is headquartered in the United States.
