## 1. Company Overview
- Speak is a language learning app designed to help users achieve fluency through conversational practice.
- Its core product is an AI-powered tutor that provides real-time feedback and personalized learning experiences.
- The target market includes individuals seeking to learn new languages, with a focus on accessibility for users globally.
- Founded in 2022, Speak is headquartered in San Francisco, California.

## 2. Business Model and GTM
- Revenue model: Subscription-based with a freemium trial option.
- Pricing: Not disclosed.
- Target customer segments: Primarily individuals (SMB) interested in language learning.
- Go-to-market approach: Focused on digital marketing and partnerships, leveraging social proof and user testimonials.
- The app is available on multiple platforms, including iOS and Android.
