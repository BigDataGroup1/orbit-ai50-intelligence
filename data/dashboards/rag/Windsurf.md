## 1. Company Overview
- Windsurf is a software development platform that leverages AI to enhance coding efficiency and streamline the development process.
- Its core product is an AI-powered integrated development environment (IDE) that assists developers with coding tasks.
- The target market includes individual developers and organizations looking to improve their software development capabilities.
- Founded in 2025, the company is headquartered in Mountain View, California.

## 2. Business Model and GTM
- Revenue model: Subscription-based with multiple pricing tiers.
- Pricing tiers:
  - Free: $0 per user/month with limited features.
  - Pro: $15 per user/month with additional features.
  - Teams: $30 per user/month with enhanced capabilities.
  - Enterprise: Custom pricing for larger organizations.
- Target customer segments include individual developers, small to medium businesses (SMBs), and enterprise clients.
- Go-to-market approach focuses on direct sales and partnerships with major IDE providers.
