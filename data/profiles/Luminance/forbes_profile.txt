Founded in 2015 by mathematicians Adam Guthrie and Graham Sills, the startup's AI helps businesses with contract generation, negotiation and contract analysis. The Cambridge-based company has raised a total of $165 million in funding, and its most recent Series C round brought in $75 million in February. Luminance—valued at $400 million, according to PitchBook—boasts over 700 customers in over 70 countries, including AMD, National Grid, Rolls-Royce and Lamborghini. In December, Luminance launched Lumi Go, a product that sends draft agreements to contract counterparties and can automatically negotiate on the sender's behalf.
