Founded in 2017 by a team of academics and entrepreneurs, Synthesia's generative AI tools are used to create corporate marketing materials as well as influencers' TikTok videos. Most recently, Synthesia crossed $100 million in annual recurring revenue and received investment backing from Adobe Ventures for an undisclosed amount.
