The service, part of CEO Varun Mohan's goal of enabling software developers to work faster and more efficiently, offers free and paid subscriptions for individual users and plans starting at $35 per month for teams and companies for the extension. In November, the company launched a new product called “the Windsurf Editor,” which helps a programmer work alongside the AI software, tracking and approving its suggestions. With enterprise traction from the likes of Zillow and Anduril, the startup has nabbed $243 million and a $1.25 billion valuation from investors including Kleiner Perkins, Greenoaks and General Catalyst.
